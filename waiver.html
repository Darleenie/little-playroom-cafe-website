Participant Waiver & Release Form

Important Notice

By signing this waiver, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to the terms and conditions outlined below. This waiver is required for all participants entering the Little Playroom Café play area.

1. Assumption of Risk

I understand that participation in activities at Little Playroom Café involves inherent risks, including but not limited to falls, collisions, and other accidents that may result in injury. I voluntarily assume all risks associated with participation.

2. Release of Liability

I hereby release, discharge, and hold harmless Little Playroom Café, its owners, employees, agents, and representatives from any and all claims, demands, actions, or causes of action arising out of or related to my participation in activities at the facility.

3. Medical Authorization

In the event of an emergency, I authorize Little Playroom Café staff to seek medical treatment for the participant if I am not present or unable to make such decisions.

4. Rules and Regulations

I agree to follow all posted rules and regulations, including but not limited to wearing appropriate footwear (grip socks), supervising children, and respecting other participants.

5. Photo Release

I grant Little Playroom Café permission to use photographs or videos taken during my visit for promotional purposes, unless I specifically request otherwise in writing.

6. Health and Safety

I confirm that the participant is in good health and not experiencing any symptoms of illness. I understand that Little Playroom Café reserves the right to deny entry to anyone with visible signs of illness.

7. Duration

This waiver remains in effect for one year from the date of signing, unless revoked in writing.

8. Age and Height Restrictions

Our playground is suitable for children up to 12 years old or 58" tall. Children must be supervised at all times.

9. Footwear Requirements

Grip socks are required for all children playing in the playground area. Regular socks are acceptable for adults. Grip socks can be purchased at the café.

10. Food and Beverage Policy

No outside food or beverages are allowed in the play area. Food and drinks must be consumed in designated café areas only.
